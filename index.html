About the Company

Built on Engineering Precision and Industrial Expertise

SD Technologies is a Bengaluru-based partnership firm founded in 2021, specializing in the manufacture and servicing of precision industrial machinery. What began as a focused CNC repair operation has grown into a full-scale manufacturing and service enterprise.

We manufacture hydraulic press brake machines, punching machines, and supply fiber laser cutting systems — serving sheet metal fabricators, automotive component makers, and general engineering firms across Karnataka and beyond. Our team of skilled engineers brings hands-on expertise to every project, from machine commissioning to ongoing maintenance contracts.
